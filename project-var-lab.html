Overview

Building for Mental Health

The Penn State Behrend Virtual Augmented Reality (VAR) Lab is an interdisciplinary research hub where students and faculty explore emerging technologies. As App Development Team Lead, I oversee the design and build of a mental health mobile application — Landing Place — aimed at supporting student wellbeing through accessible, interactive tools.

The project runs on React Native with Expo Go, enabling cross-platform deployment from a single codebase. My role sits at the intersection of engineering and project management: writing code alongside the team while coordinating timelines, tracking milestones, and ensuring consistent UX quality across every release.

My Role & Responsibilities

What I Do on This Project

  • Lead app development initiatives, overseeing the full software development lifecycle from requirements through testing and deployment.
  • Manage and mentor a team of 5 programmers — conducting code reviews and ensuring adherence to quality standards.
  • Own project pacing and milestone tracking, adjusting scope and priorities to keep the team on schedule.
  • Architect feature implementations and resolve complex technical blockers across the codebase.
  • Test, debug, and optimize code for performance, accessibility, and UX consistency across iOS and Android.
  • Collaborate with faculty advisors and stakeholders to align technical output with research goals.

Technical Highlights

Under the Hood

The application leverages React Native's component model for a modular, maintainable codebase. Expo Go accelerates the development feedback loop — allowing rapid iteration across physical devices without native build overhead. State management and navigation are handled through established React Native patterns, keeping the architecture clean as the project grows.

User experience and accessibility are core design constraints. The mental health context demands interfaces that are calm, intuitive, and trustworthy. Every screen goes through functional testing before milestone delivery.
